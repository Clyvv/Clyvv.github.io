About

Established in 2017, World Trade Networks is a 100% blackowned firm with directors having extensive experiences in different fields.

World Trade Networks is a dynamic company which focuses on facilitating trade across Africa. We offer a broad spectrum of services through our highly competent consultants customized to suit your specific business request.Our primary focus involves facilitating order financing, import and export services, shipping and clearing services and also advisory services to companies who would like to pursue business adventures in Zimbabwe, South Africa and Botswana. We have partnered with different organisations to provide you with the best solution.
